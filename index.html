Key Findings

Three Critical Insights

Motion Over Static

Tracking motion dynamics over static hand shapes to avoid confusing similar signs.

Low Latency

Crucial for real-time conversation. Slow frame rates miss fast signs.

Flexible Distance

50cm range is too restrictive, recognition must work reliably
at any distance.
